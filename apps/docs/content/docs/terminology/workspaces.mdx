## What are Workspaces?

Workspaces are the top-level organizational units in Kaneo. They serve as containers for projects, tasks, and team members. Think of a workspace as a dedicated space for a specific team, department, or area of work.

## Creating a Workspace

To create a new workspace:

<Steps>
<Step>
Click on the workspace selector in the top-left corner
</Step>
<Step>
Select "Create Workspace"
</Step>
<Step>
Enter a name for your workspace
</Step>
<Step>
Click "Create"
</Step>
</Steps>


## Managing Workspace Settings

Each workspace has its own settings that you can configure:

<Steps>
<Step>
Click on the workspace selector in the top-left corner
</Step>
<Step>
Select the workspace you want to manage
</Step>
<Step>
Click on "Settings" in the sidebar
</Step>
</Steps>

From the settings page, you can:
- Update the workspace name
- Configure workspace-specific settings

## Switching Between Workspaces

If you belong to multiple workspaces, you can easily switch between them:

<Steps>
<Step>
Click on the workspace selector in the top-left corner
</Step>
<Step>
Select the workspace you want to switch to
</Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Workspace

To delete a workspace:

<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to the workspace settings
</Step>
<Step>
Scroll to the bottom of the page
</Step>
<Step>
Click on "Delete Workspace"
</Step>
<Step>
Confirm the deletion
</Step>
</Steps>
**Note**: Deleting a workspace will permanently remove all projects, tasks, and data associated with it. This action cannot be undone.
