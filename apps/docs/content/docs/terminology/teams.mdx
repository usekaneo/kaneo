## Team Management in Kaneo

Kaneo allows you to collaborate with team members across workspaces and projects. Effective team management is essential for successful collaboration.

## Inviting Team Members

To invite someone to your workspace:
<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to the workspace settings
</Step>

<Step>
Click on the "Members" tab
</Step>

<Step>
Click "Invite Member"
</Step>

<Step>
Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
</Step>

<Step>
Select the appropriate role for the new member
</Step>

<Step>
Click "Send Invitation"
</Step>
</Steps>

The invited person will have access to the workspace after logging in. ðŸŽ‰

## Managing Team Members

To manage existing team members:
<Steps>
<Step>
Navigate to the workspace settings
</Step>

<Step>
Click on the "Members" tab
</Step>

<Step>
From here, you can:
   - Add a member to the workspace
   - Remove a member from the workspace
</Step>
</Steps>
